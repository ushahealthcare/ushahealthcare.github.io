About Us

Who are We?

We are a nursing service agency ready to provide you satisfactory services at your door step. This is a service offered to patients suffering from chronic illness, disabled, senile and whose mobility is restricted within four walls. Our nursing service is to provide attenders to those people who are hospitalized and who need their treatment at home along with their family members. We offer for 8 hours Day and Night shifts 24*7 and monthly basis nurse/female attenders are also available. Any other specific requirements are also provided as per the patient.
We also provide Baby Care and Old Age Home service facilities.
